About Winetrekking Portugal

...short story about how to enjoy wine with the view

Portugal has its spectacular beauty that everyone should discover, but of course the best to do it it is exploring the nature here by walking. This country is full of many different and beautiful trails, located in every part of the country. We are focused mostly on Lisbon region, Madeira island and the archipelago Azores. We always make sure that during the hiking/trekking we will be able to see as much of amazing spots and view points as possible, where we can sit for a while and appreciate the views with a nice glass of a local Portuguese wine. To complete the experience there is always a plate of snacks served where you can find some good Portuguese cheese, bread and olives.
